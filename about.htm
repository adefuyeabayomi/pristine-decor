Briefly About Us

We are an interior design and decoration company based in Lagos, Nigeria. We started operation in the year 2018 with a small team and have grown steadily since then. We operate in mainly in Lagos but we also take projects in Ogun State and Oyo state. We Also offer online services to Our clients who are far away. We work by our principles of hardwork, client satisfaction and excellence.

Our Mission

We have a mission to positively impact the culture and trends in Nigeria with our designs. We also want to give training and equip to upcoming Designers in Nigeria.

Our Vision

Expanding to all parts of Nigeria and West Africa. We aim to become a pacesetter of style and excellence in everyplace we operate in.
